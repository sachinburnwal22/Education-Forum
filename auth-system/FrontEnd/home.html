    Joining a study group is easy! Navigate to the "Study Groups" section, browse available groups by subject or course code, and click "Join Group." You can also create your own group if you don't find one that matches your needs.

    EduConnect offers several powerful collaboration tools:

    • Real-time document collaboration with version history
    • Virtual study rooms with video, audio, and screen sharing
    • Smart resource sharing with automatic categorization
    • Task management for group projects
    • Discussion threads organized by topic

    Our AI-powered matching system analyzes your academic profile, courses, and learning preferences to suggest compatible study partners. The algorithm considers:

    Course overlap
    Learning style
    Availability
